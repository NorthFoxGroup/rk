Learn the latest steps of Garba and Lose Weight.

Garba is a form of dance which originates from the state of Gujarat in India.

  • Unique garba steps with quality teaching and highly experienced crew.
  • Learn from basic techniques to advance style by well planned sessions.
  • Pleasent ambience and High quality sound system at affordable price.

Garba is a Gujarati folk dance celebrated in Navratri, a celebration lasting nine nights. Garba songs typically revolve around the subjects of the nine goddesses. Garba styles vary from place to place in Gujarat.
